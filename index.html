Data Sources

Primary Dataset: County Health Rankings & Roadmaps - 2025 Release (v3)
Provides 2024 health outcomes, health behaviors, clinical care, and socioeconomic data for 3,159 U.S. counties. A collaboration between the Robert Wood Johnson Foundation and the University of Wisconsin Population Health Institute.

Additional Datasets Available:

  • CDC PLACES (Local Data for Better Health): County-level health data for years 2018-2022
  • County Health Rankings Trends: Historical data from 2014-2021 for select metrics

Note: The time series functionality in the county click modal (2022-2024) currently uses synthetic data based on 2024 baseline values. Integration of the historical PLACES and CHR Trends datasets is planned for future development to provide real year-over-year comparisons.
